Key Livermore Principles in This Strategy

Breakout Entry Buy only when price breaks above the 20-day high — confirming the line of least resistance is upward.
Trend Confirmation Only trade when price is above both the 50MA and 200MA — never fight the major trend.
Exit Discipline Exit immediately when price breaks below the 20-day low — cut losses without hesitation.
No Averaging Down Trades only in the direction of strength — never hold a losing position hoping for recovery.
